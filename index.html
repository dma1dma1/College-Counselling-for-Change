Welcome to College Counselling for Change!

Who Are We?

We are a social advocacy initiative started by Ward Melville Alumni. Our goal is to integrate social advocacy with the next generation of college students. We drive donations to our three vetted charities in exchange for one-on-one college counseling sessions.

We believe education is the cornerstone to change. We invest in you by helping you start the college application process, and we invest in underprivileged communities through your donations and by providing our services to them for free.

We are students at Harvard, Princeton, Stanford, CMU, CalTech, UMich, Stony Brook and more!

Learn more about us in counselors!

We Help Students

  • Plan where and when to apply
  • Create a timeline and system to stay organized
  • Use resources compiled by Ward Melville Alumni
  • Navigate Application Portals
  • Use personal advice for their application process

Where Your Contribution Will Go

When you sign up with us, you will be asked to make a donation to one of

  • Erase Racism NY
  • Teach For America
  • Brooklyn Community Fund

Learn more about each charity and how to donate in charities, or check out our comprehensive donation guide.

Panels - Free!

Our free, virtual panels will occur between July 14th and July 21st!

As almost all colleges have canceled tours due to COVID-19, it becomes increasingly hard for you to understand the intangibles of a campus. Our panels are designed to help you guys understand the social atmosphere, campus life and quality of life of our colleges. Free and open for everyone.
